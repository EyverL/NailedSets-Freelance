Health and Safety

  1. Severly damaged nails cannot be worked on and will result in
    a cancellation/reschedule. If you are unsure, please send a picture
    along with your reservation form.

Policies

  1. $15 non-refundable deposit required.
  2. 10 min grace period for all appointments. I can only
    wait 10 minutes after your appointment was suppose to start
    before having to cancel/reschedule
  3. Please notify me at least 24 hrs before your appointment
    if you need to reschedule to avoid cancellation fees.
  4. No extra guests unless previously approved and absolutely
    no small children are allowed in the salon.
  5. No call, no show or same day cancellations will result in
    a $20 cancellation fee.
  6. First time clients must pay remaining balance in cash. We
    have no ATM's so please plan accordingly.
  7. Please text me upon arrival to the salon. Our doors remain
    locked so I will need to open the door for your.
